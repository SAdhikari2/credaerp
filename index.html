Frequently Asked Questions

How does the 3-month free trial work?
You can sign up for any of our three plans (Starter, Growth, or Premium) and receive full access completely free for the first 3 months. No credit card or contract is required. We want to ensure that ArogyaPOS is the perfect fit for your pharmacy before any billing begins.
How is the license activated for my local system?
ArogyaPOS uses high-security offline activation. During signup, your hardware identifier (fingerprint) is linked to an RSA-signed license key. You paste this key into the app once, and it will authenticate locally. This design ensures that your checkout counter is fully functional even during local internet outages.
What are the subscription billing cycles?
We offer Quarterly and Yearly subscription cycles for the Indian and global market. There are no monthly commitments, which allows us to lower operating and collection costs, passing these savings directly to you. Yearly plans include a savings discount of up to 25%.
Can I change plans at a later time?
Yes, you can upgrade, downgrade, or switch billing cycles (quarterly to yearly) at any time. When upgrading, new features like E-Invoicing, Day Closure, or Advanced Reports are unlocked instantly. Downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing period.
What is E-Invoicing (IRN) support?
In India, B2B sales invoices for pharmacies above statutory thresholds must generate an IRN (Invoice Reference Number) and a signed QR code on the bill. The Premium plan automates this, generating the compliance payload and sending it directly to the Invoice Registration Portal (IRP) in real-time.
What happens when the 3-month trial ends?
Near the end of your trial, we'll notify you inside the application. To keep your POS terminal active, you can subscribe on a quarterly or yearly cycle. If you decide not to continue, your account will be paused, and no charges will apply.
